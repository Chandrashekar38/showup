# ShowUp Vision

## Mission

Help people build a permanent record of consistency by documenting the commitments they make and the progress they achieve over time.

## Core Belief

Most platforms celebrate outcomes.

ShowUp celebrates showing up.

## Philosophy

The goal matters.

Showing up matters more.

Success is an outcome.

Consistency is a habit.

## Long-Term Vision

Create a place where people can look back years later and see exactly how they became who they are.
